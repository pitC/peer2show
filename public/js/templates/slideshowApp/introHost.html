Convenience

We keep it simple and free. You do not need to sign-up to create a session . Just give in your name or nickname, drag&drop images and send a link to your friends. Experience a real-time good quality slideshow. As you navigate through your show, current slide is displayed to all viewers, so you can be sure everyone stays on the same page.

Real-time

The images are not uploaded to any server. This way you make sure that you do not handle the rights to your pictures to anyone. They are also not stored in the cloud - everything happens just between you and the invited person!

Privacy & Safety

You control who sees the pictures. As a user joins the session, you see a notification in the right top corner. There is no way somebody forwards the link and you do not realise that somebody else is watching the pictures. Isn't that delightfully simple compared to other available options?
